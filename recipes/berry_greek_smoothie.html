Berry Greek Smoothie

Description

A creamy, tangy-sweet smoothie packed with protein from Greek yogurt and antioxidants from fresh berries — refreshing, filling, and perfect for a quick snack or breakfast.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
  • ½ cup milk (or almond milk)
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional, for texture)

Steps

  1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass and enjoy.
