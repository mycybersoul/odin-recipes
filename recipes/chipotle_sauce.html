Chipotle Yogurt Sauce

Description

Blend Greek yogurt, canned chipotle peppers in adobo, a squeeze of lime juice, garlic powder, and a pinch of salt. It makes a smoky, tangy, creamy sauce that’s great for tacos, bowls, or as a dip.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1–2 chipotle peppers in adobo (plus 1 tsp of the adobo sauce)
  • 1 clove garlic (or ½ tsp garlic powder)
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender or food processor.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Taste and adjust: more lime for tang, more chipotle for heat.
